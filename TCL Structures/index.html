About

Building Excellence Together

TCL is a leading construction company specializing in construction concrete, groundworks, and drainage services. Our team consists of highly skilled concreting operatives, ground-workers, steel fixers, and formwork joiners who are dedicated to delivering top-quality results on every project.

With a wealth of experience in the industry, TCL ensures that all construction projects are completed to the highest standards of craftsmanship. Whether it's building foundations, creating structural elements, or managing intricate drainage systems, our team is equipped to handle projects of all sizes with precision and efficiency. Trust TCL for all your construction needs.

Our Services

Building Dreams with TCL Structures

TCL is your top choice for construction needs with expertise in concrete works, ground and drainage projects, skilled workers, and concrete pump hire services. We ensure top-notch quality and timely completion of projects, providing exceptional value to every client. Our dedicated team delivers professional results, making TCL the ideal partner for all your construction requirements.
Concrete Works
Our main area of expertise lies in the construction of in situ concrete structures. These include ground bearing slabs, structural frames, retaining walls, watertight structures, basement and more.
Ground & Drainage
Our main area of expertise lies in the construction of in situ concrete structures. These include ground bearing slabs, structural frames, retaining walls, watertight structures, basement and more.
Concrete Pump Hire
Supply concrete boom pumps all over the UK. The sizes we offer are 24m, 36m and 42m. All our operators have NPORS and CPCS qualifications and extensive experience.
Plant & Grab Hire
We provide quality and affordable tipper and grab hire in Wolverhampton, Walsall, Dudley, Cannock, and Birmingham. We can provide tippers and grabs for both domestic and commercial waste or soil removal.
